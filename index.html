What is 'Title Verification' and why is it necessary?

Before purchasing, we conduct a search for the last 30 years to ensure the property is free from encumbrances, litigation, or hidden liabilities. This protects your investment from future legal claims.

How does Conveyancing work?

Conveyancing is the legal process of transferring ownership. It involves drafting the Sale Deed, paying the appropriate Stamp Duty, and ensuring Registration at the Sub-Registrar's office.
